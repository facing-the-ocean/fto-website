Get Involved

FtO is open, community-driven, and free to participate in. Here's how.

🎟️

Attend an Event

FtO events are held annually across the Asia-Pacific — open to civic hackers of all kinds. Developers, designers, researchers, policy people, and anyone who wants to work on civic challenges.

💡

Propose a Project

Have a civic tech idea you'd like to work on with others? Bring it to FtO. A 3-minute pitch on Day 1 is all you need to recruit collaborators from across the region.

🏙️

Host an Event

FtO rotates between cities in Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan. If your community wants to host a future edition, connect with us on GitHub or through your local civic tech community.

🛠️

Contribute

FtO's documentation and website are open source. Improve docs on HackMD, submit PRs on GitHub, translate content, or connect us with local civic tech communities.
