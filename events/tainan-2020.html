About the Event

FtO Tainan 2020 brought the event back to Tainan, co-hosted alongside the g0v biannual summit. The event was held at A Break Cafe — a unique café-style hacking environment open to everyone. Participants were invited to sign up, propose projects, and come hack together in Tainan's creative atmosphere.
