About the Event

FtO Jeju 2023 was held on the beautiful island of Jeju — South Korea's most-visited island, known for its volcanic landscape and ocean views. The venue was the NIA Global Center in Seogwipo City, about 10 minutes by car from the city centre.

Participants arrived from Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, and beyond for three days of civic hacking, collaboration, and community building.
