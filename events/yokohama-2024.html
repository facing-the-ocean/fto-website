About the Event

FtO Yokohama 2024 was hosted by Code for Japan at PACIFICO Yokohama — one of Japan's premier convention centres — co-located with a major civic tech event. The event featured collaborative workshops, hacking sessions, and emergency response coordination during a typhoon warning period.

Workshops

The event included two dedicated workshop sessions exploring civic tech themes. A Ground Rules / Code of Conduct workshop was also held, feeding into the FtO CoC development process.
